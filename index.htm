HOW TO BUY

Create a Wallet

Download Phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension.

Get Some SOL

Have SOL in your wallet to switch. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on Phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Jupiter or Radium

Connect to any of the above exchanges. Paste the token address into your exchange, select Bat Frog, and confirm. When it prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Switch SOL for Bat Frog

Switch SOL for Bat Frog. We have ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
